ABOUT US

Founded in October of 2003 and based in Dehradun, Ashoka Jewellers and Sons have built a trusted marketplace for people to buy jewellery in Dehradun.

Whether you want a diamond necklace, an engagement ring, bracelets or ear-rings, we have them all customized especially for our valued customers. Since this is our ancestral business we have a history of trust, respect and understanding with our customers. Along with that our owner Mr. Amit Kansal is a government approved valuer, and a member of GJEPC (Gem & Jewellery Export Promotion Council). He is also a licensed Exporter of Gold and Silver jewellery.
